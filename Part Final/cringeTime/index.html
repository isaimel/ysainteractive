Will I ever hold your hand
Through this somber, gray land
Can you guide these blinded eyes
Into our joint paradise?

Will I ever have this dance
Oh so steeped in romance
Can you let my arm lay
on your hips as we sway

Will I ever hear your cry
Your laughs and your goodbye
Can you set my mind at ease
And please return to me

Can you bless me with your light
Can you hold me firm and strong
To still my sunken heart
And show me where I belong
